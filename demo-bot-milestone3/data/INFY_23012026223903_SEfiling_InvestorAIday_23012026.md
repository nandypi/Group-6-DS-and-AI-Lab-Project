# Investor AI Day

Infosys Limited will hold an Investor AI Day in Bengaluru on February 17, 2026. The agenda of the event will be finalized in due course.

The proceedings of the event will be webcast live on the company's website. The presentations made at the event and transcripts of discussions will also be hosted on the company's website subsequently.
