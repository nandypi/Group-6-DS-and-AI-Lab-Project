# Group-6-DS-and-AI-Lab-Project

Group 6 DS and AI Lab Project

Retail and SME investors in the Indian capital markets often face a significant information and interpretation gap despite having access to the same public disclosures as institutional investors. Corporate announcements, stock exchange filings, earnings reports, and regulatory circulars are released continuously and contain complex financial, legal, and operational information that can be difficult to interpret quickly.

Large institutional investors deploy dedicated research teams and sophisticated market intelligence platforms to continuously monitor and analyze such disclosures in real time. Commercial solutions provide similar capabilities, but their cost makes them inaccessible to most retail and SME investors. As a result, smaller market participants frequently face delays in understanding the significance of new developments and their potential impact on investment portfolios.

There is therefore a need for an affordable and intelligent system that can assist investors in understanding corporate disclosures and regulatory updates more efficiently. This project aims to develop a system capable of processing public source documents (such as PDF reports and exchange filings), utilizing computer vision to parse tabular financial data from scanned documents, and applying natural language processing and generative AI frameworks to filter noise and generate concise text summaries. The final processed insights will be made available through a simplified web dashboard interface to support more informed investment decisions.
