Infosys is to deploy its Topaz Fabric platform, a purpose-built agentic services suite, to
help Sentara scale AI use cases from pilot programs to full production across
enterprise systems. The approach will also help the not-for-profit healthcare
organization prioritize high-value use cases, scale successful pilots, and evolve AI
capabilities across business operations.
