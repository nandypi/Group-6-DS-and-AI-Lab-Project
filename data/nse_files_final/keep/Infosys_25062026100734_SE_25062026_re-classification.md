## TO ALL STOCK EXCHANGES

## BSE LIMITED NATIONAL STOCK EXCHANGE OF INDIA LIMITED NEW YORK STOCK EXCHANGE

June 25, 2026

Dear Sir/ Madam,

Subject:  Intimation  of  approval  of  shareholders  for  reclassification  of  certain  members  of  the promoter and promoter group under Regulation 31A read with Regulation 30 of the SEBI (Listing Obligations and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 2015

This is in furtherance of our disclosures dated April 30, 2026, May 1, 2026 and May 11, 2026, we wish to inform you that in terms of Regulation 31A of the SEBI (Listing Obligations and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 2015 (' Listing Regulations '), the shareholders of the Company, in the 45 th  Annual General Meeting held on Tuesday, June 23, 2026, have approved the proposal of re-classification of Mr. Shreyas Shibulal and Ms. Bhairavi Madhusudhan Shibulal ('the Applicants') from the category of 'Promoter and Promoter Group' to 'Public'.

The  Company  has  intimated  the  results  of  the  voting  to  all  relevant  authorities  to  give  effect  to  the reclassification,  in  accordance  with  the  applicable  laws.  Therefore,  the  Applicants  stand  reclassified  to 'Public' category with immediate effect.

The same will also be made available on the Company's website www.infosys.com.

This is for your information and records.

Yours Sincerely, For

Infosys Limited

A.G.S. Manikantha Company Secretary Membership No: A21918
