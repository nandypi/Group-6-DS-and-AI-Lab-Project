# Disclosure Pursuant to Regulation 30 of the SEBI Listing Regulations

March 31, 2026

Infosys Limited ("Company") has received assessment orders under Section 250 and Section 254 of the Income-tax Act, 1961 from the Income-tax Department, Government of India.

The orders were received for Assessment Years 2018-19 and 2017-18 on March 26, 2026, and March 27, 2026, respectively, and for Assessment Years 2013-14, 2019-20, 2020-21 and 2021-22 on March 31, 2026.

As per the said orders, the Company expects a cumulative refund of ₹1,745 crore, including interest. The Company is currently evaluating the impact of these orders on its financial statements for the quarter and year ended March 31, 2026.
