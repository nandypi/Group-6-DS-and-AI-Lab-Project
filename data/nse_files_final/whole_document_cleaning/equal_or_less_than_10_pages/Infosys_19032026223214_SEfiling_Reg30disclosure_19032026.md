# Intimation Under Regulation 30 of SEBI Listing Regulations

In continuation to the letter dated March 18, 2026 regarding incorporating a step-down wholly-owned subsidiary, Infosys Limited has completed incorporating a step-down wholly-owned subsidiary in the name and style of Infosys Enterprise Business Services Pty. Ltd in Australia.
