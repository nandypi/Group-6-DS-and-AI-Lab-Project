# Intimation Under Regulation 30 of the SEBI Listing Regulations

In continuation to the letter dated 5th December, 2025, it is hereby informed that the merger between two wholly owned step-down subsidiaries, In-tech Engineering services S.R.L Romania and ProIT S.R.L.RO Romania, was completed on January 8, 2026.

The Company received this information on January 14, 2026.

The name of the resultant entity is In-tech Engineering S.R.L.
