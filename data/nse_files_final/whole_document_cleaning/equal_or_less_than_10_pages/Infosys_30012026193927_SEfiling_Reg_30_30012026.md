# Intimation under Regulation 30 of SEBI Listing Regulations

In continuation to the letter dated November 14, 2025, Infosys Limited informed that it has completed the transfer of 90% of the shareholding held in Infosys Consulting S.R.L., Argentina, a majority owned subsidiary, to Infosys Nova Holdings LLC, a wholly owned subsidiary of the Company, on January 28, 2026.

The Company received this information on January 29, 2026.
