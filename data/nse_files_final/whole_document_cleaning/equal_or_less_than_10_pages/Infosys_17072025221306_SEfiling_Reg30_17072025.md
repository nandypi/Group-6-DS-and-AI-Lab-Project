# Company Update

July 17, 2025

The Board of Infosys BPM UK Limited, a step-down wholly owned subsidiary of Infosys Limited, has approved the incorporation of a wholly owned subsidiary in the name and style of Infosys BPM Canada Inc. or by any other name as available in the commercial register, in Canada.

Additional information as required under Regulation 30 of SEBI (Listing Obligations and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 2015 will be disclosed in due course.
