# Company Update

December 09, 2025

The Board of Infosys Singapore Pte Ltd, a wholly owned subsidiary of Infosys Limited, has approved the incorporation of a wholly owned subsidiary in the name and style of "Infosys Vietnam LLC" or by any other name as available in the corporate registry in Vietnam.

Additional information as required under Regulation 30 of SEBI (Listing Obligations and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 2015 will be disclosed in due course.
