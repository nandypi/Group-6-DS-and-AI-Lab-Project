# Infosys Announces Expansion of Mohali Development Center

Infosys strengthens its regional footprint with a new 350,000-sq. ft. integrated development campus in Mohali.

Bengaluru, India - March 12, 2026: Infosys (NSE, BSE, NYSE: INFY), a global leader in next-generation digital services and consulting, today announced the expansion of its Mohali Development Center (DC) with a formal groundbreaking ceremony held at the campus, further strengthening its presence in North India. With this expansion, Infosys reaffirms its commitment to driving innovation, fostering local talent, and contributing to the economic and technological growth of Punjab.

The ceremony was graced by Hon'ble Chief Minister of Punjab, S. Bhagwant Singh Mann and Hon'ble Cabinet Minister for Investment Promotion, Power and Local Government for Punjab, Shri. Sanjeev Arora, in the presence of Infosys leadership represented by Mr. Jayesh Sanghrajka, Chief Financial Officer, along with Mr. Sunil Kumar Dhareshwar, Executive Vice President, Infosys, Mr. Niladri Prasad Mishra, Senior Vice President, Infosys, and other senior officials from the State Government and Infosys.

As part of this expansion, Infosys will develop a new Software Development Block along with supporting facilities. The expansion is planned to have a built-up area of approximately 350,000 square feet and will seat about 3,000 employees, strengthening Infosys' ability to design, co-create, and deploy AI-led solutions at enterprise scale.

## Key Highlights of the DC Expansion

- A high-efficiency building, naturally daylit interiors, intelligent LED-based lighting with daylight harvesting and occupancy sensors
- Advanced radiant cooling and heating supported by zone-based HVAC controls
- A comprehensive water-saving and 100% wastewater recycling system using Membrane Bioreactor technology
- Extensive green landscaping to reduce heat-island effects, eco-friendly construction materials with modular systems to minimize wastes
- Smart building technologies that enable continuous monitoring and performance optimization

The current operations at Infosys Mohali, with close to 1,000 employees, delivers advanced digital services to global clients across key sectors, including Banking, Financial Services and Insurance, Retail, and Healthcare. The expanded campus will further enhance Infosys' capabilities in cloud, AI, application development, and digital transformation, enabling the company to better meet the growing demands of its global clients and help them unlock tangible AI-driven value at scale.

S. Bhagwant Singh Mann, Hon'ble Chief Minister of Punjab, said, "The expansion of Infosys' Mohali DC strengthens Punjab's position as an emerging hub for technology and innovation. This investment will create significant employment opportunities for the youth of Punjab and neighboring states while bolstering the state's IT ecosystem. We look forward to Infosys' continued efforts in driving technology-led and inclusive economic growth in the region."

Mr. Jayesh Sanghrajka, Chief Financial Officer, Infosys, said, "We are delighted to expand our presence in Mohali, marking an important milestone in advancing our AI-first strategy to deliver greater value to clients. This integrated development enables us to leverage strong local talent, enhance operational agility, and provide our employees with a flexible hybrid work environment. The expansion will also contribute to the economic growth of Punjab by creating high-quality employment opportunities and strengthening the region's technology ecosystem."
