# Intimation Under Regulation 30 of SEBI Listing Regulations

In continuation to the letter dated June 12, 2025, Infosys Limited informed that the merger between two wholly owned step-down subsidiaries, Infosys Germany GmbH and Blitz 24-893 SE, has been completed on September 24, 2025.

The name of the resultant entity is Infosys Germany SE.
