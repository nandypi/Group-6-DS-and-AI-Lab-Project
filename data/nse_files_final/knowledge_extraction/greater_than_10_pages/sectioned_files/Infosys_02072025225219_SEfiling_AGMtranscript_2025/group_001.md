Infosys grew 4.2% in constant currency, delivering US$19.2 billion in revenues. Operating margin for the year expanded by 50 basis points to 21.1% and we generated US$4.1 billion in free cash flow, which is a record increase of 41.8% over fiscal 2024. Large deal TCV for FY25 had sustained momentum at US$11.6 billion. The Company's robust performance reflects the strength of our client relationships, which will continue to be at the heart of all our future endeavors. Everything we do is designed to earn the trust and respect of our stakeholders worldwide. At Infosys, our purpose, the value we bring to everyone around us, is to amplify human potential and create the next opportunity for people, businesses and communities.
