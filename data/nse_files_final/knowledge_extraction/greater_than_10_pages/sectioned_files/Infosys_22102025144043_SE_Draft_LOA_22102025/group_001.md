## Sub: Update on proposal for Buyback of Equity Shares of the Company
This has reference to our letter dated September 11, 2025, regarding the outcome of the meeting of the Board of Directors of Infosys Limited (' Company ') approving the proposal to buyback equity shares for an amount of ₹ 18,000 Crore (Rupees Eighteen Thousand Crore only) (' Buyback Size ') at a price of ₹ 1,800/ -(Rupees One Thousand Eight Hundred only) per Equity Share (" Buyback Price "), payable in cash, comprising of a purchase of 10,00,00,000 fully paidup equity shares of the Company of face value of ₹ 5/- each (' Equity Shares ') representing up to 2.41% of the total number of Equity Shares in the existing total paidup Equity Share capital of the Company (on a standalone basis), from the equity shareholders of the Company as on a record date to be announced later (' Record Date ').
