## 2.25 Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR)
As per Section 135 of the Companies Act, 2013, a company, meeting the applicability threshold, needs to spend at least 2% of its average net profit for the immediately preceding three financial years on corporate social responsibility (CSR) activities. The areas for CSR activities are education, skilling &amp; livelihoods, environment sustainability and ecological balance, healthcare, including preventive health and others (promotion of national heritage, art and culture, rural development and disaster relief and rehabilitation). A CSR committee has been formed by the Company as per the Act.
