## TO ALL STOCK EXCHANGES

## BSE LIMITED NATIONAL STOCK EXCHANGE OF INDIA LIMITED NEW YORK STOCK EXCHANGE

July 17, 2025

Dear Sir/ Madam,

## Sub: Company update

This is to inform that, Board of Infosys BPM UK Limited, a step-down wholly owned subsidiary of Infosys Limited  has  approved  to  incorporate  a  wholly  owned  subsidiary  in  the  name  and  style  of  Infosys  BPM Canada Inc. (or by any other name as available in the commercial register) in Canada.  Additional information as required under Regulation 30 of SEBI (Listing Obligations and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 2015 will be disclosed in due course.

This  is  for  your  information  and  records.  The  same  will  also  be  hosted  on  the  Company's  website,  at www.infosys.com

Thanking you,

Yours sincerely,

For Infosys Limited

A.G.S. Manikantha Company Secretary
