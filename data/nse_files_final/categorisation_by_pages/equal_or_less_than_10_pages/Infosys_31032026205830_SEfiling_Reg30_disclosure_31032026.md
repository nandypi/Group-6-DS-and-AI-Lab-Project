## TO ALL STOCK EXCHANGES

## BSE LIMITED NATIONAL STOCK EXCHANGE OF INDIA LIMITED NEW YORK STOCK EXCHANGE

Dear Sir / Madam,

Sub: Disclosure pursuant to Regulation 30 of the SEBI (Listing Obligations and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 2015 ('Listing Regulations')

Infosys Limited ('Company') has received assessment orders under Section 250 and Section 254 of the Income-tax Act, 1961 from the Income-tax Department, Government of India. The orders were received  for  Assessment  Years  2018-19  and  2017-18  on  March  26,  2026,  and  March  27,  2026, respectively, and for Assessment Years 2013-14, 2019-20, 2020-21 and 2021-22 on March 31, 2026.

As per the said orders, the Company expects a cumulative refund of ₹1, 745 crore (including interest). The Company is currently evaluating the impact of these orders on its financial statements for the quarter and year ended March 31, 2026.

This is for your information and records.

This will also be hosted on the Company's website, at www.infosys.com

Yours sincerely, For

Infosys Limited
