## TO ALL STOCK EXCHANGES

BSE LIMITED NATIONAL STOCK EXCHANGE OF INDIA LIMITED NEW YORK STOCK EXCHANGE

Dear Sir / Madam,

Sub:  Intimation  under  Regulation  30  of  the  SEBI  (Listing  Obligations  and  Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 2015

In continuation to our letter dated 5 th  December, 2025, it is hereby informed that the merger between two wholly owned step down subsidiaries viz, In-tech Engineering services S.R.L Romania and ProIT S.R.L.RO Romania was completed on January 8, 2026. The Company received this information on January 14, 2026. The name of the resultant entity is In-tech Engineering S.R.L.

This is for your information and records.

This will also be hosted on the Company's website, at www.infosys.com
