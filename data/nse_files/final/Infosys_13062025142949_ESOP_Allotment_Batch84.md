## TO ALL STOCK EXCHANGES

## BSE LIMITED NATIONAL STOCK EXCHANGE OF INDIA LIMITED NEW YORK STOCK EXCHANGE

June 13, 2025

Dear Sir/Madam,

## Subject: Allotment of Equity Shares

This is to inform you that the Company has allotted 59,314 equity shares of face value of ₹ 5 each vide a Board resolution dated June 12, 2025, and approved on June 13, 2025, pursuant to the exercise of Restricted Stock Units by eligible employees as hereunder:

- 3,894 equity shares under the 2015 Incentive Compensation Plan;
- 55,420 equity shares under the Infosys Expanded Stock Ownership Program 2019.

Consequently, effective from June 13, 2025, the issued and subscribed share capital of the Company stands increased to ₹ 20,77,13,63,140/- divided into 4,15,42,72,628 equity shares of face value of ₹ 5/- each.

This is for your information and records.

This will also be hosted on the Company's website, at www.infosys.com.

Yours Sincerely, For Infosys Limited

## A.G.S. Manikantha

Company Secretary Membership No. A21918
