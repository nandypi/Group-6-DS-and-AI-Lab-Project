## NATIONAL STOCK EXCHANGE OF INDIA LIMITED

June 5, 2025

Dear Sir/Madam,

## Subject: Clarification letter

## Re: Your email dated June 2, 2025, regarding Clarification for Financial results - INFY

We wish  to  inform  you  that,  the  clause  being  referred  is  under  Reg  33  (3)(e)  is  replicated  below  for reference:

The listed entity shall also submit the audited or limited reviewed financial results in respect of the last quarter along-with the results for the entire financial year, with a note stating that the figures of last quarter are the balancing figures between audited figures in respect of the full financial year and the published year-to-date figures upto the third quarter of the current financial year

Our response - As per the SEBI clause a note has to be given stating that the last quarter figures (Q4) is the balancing figure of year to date figures for the full year and the figures published till Q3 of that year. Infosys presents audited quarterly financial results to the stock exchange every quarter. Every quarterly result is based on audited financials for that quarter and year to date figures including the last quarter (Q4). The Q4 results are not balancing figures rather they are based on audited financial statements for the quarter ended March 31, 2025. Accordingly, since the note mentioned is Reg 33 3(e) is not applicable to the company the company has not provided the same in its filing with the stock exchange. Also this practice has been followed by the company consistently year on year.

Yours sincerely,

For Infosys Limited
