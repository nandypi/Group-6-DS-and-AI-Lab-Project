## TO ALL STOCK EXCHANGES

## BSE LIMITED NATIONAL STOCK EXCHANGE OF INDIA LIMITED NEW YORK STOCK EXCHANGE

February 20, 2026

Dear Sir/Madam,

## Subject: Allotment of Equity Shares

This is to inform you that the Company has allotted 7,16,314 equity shares of face value of ₹ 5 each vide a Board resolution dated February 19, 2026, and approved on February 20, 2026, pursuant to the exercise of Restricted Stock Units by eligible employees as hereunder:

- 3,77,110 equity shares under the 2015 Incentive Compensation Plan;
- 3,39,204 equity shares under the Infosys Expanded Stock Ownership Program 2019.

Consequently,  effective  from  February  20,  2026,  the  issued  and  subscribed  share  capital  of  the Company stands increased to ₹ 20,27,69,55,635/- divided into 4,05,53,91,127 equity shares of face value of ₹ 5/- each.

This is for your information and records.

This will also be hosted on the Company's website, at www.infosys.com.

Yours Sincerely, For

Infosys Limited
