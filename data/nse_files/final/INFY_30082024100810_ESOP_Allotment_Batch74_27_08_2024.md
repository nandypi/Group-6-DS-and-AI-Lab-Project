## TO ALL STOCK EXCHANGES

## BSE LIMITED NATIONAL STOCK EXCHANGE OF INDIA LIMITED NEW YORK STOCK EXCHANGE

August 30, 2024

Dear Sir/Madam,

## Subject: Allotment of Equity Shares

This is to inform you that the Company has allotted 19,729 equity shares of face value of ₹ 5 each vide a  Board resolution dated August 27, 2024 and approved on August 29, 2024, pursuant to the exercise of Restricted Stock Units by eligible employees as hereunder:

- 17,087 equity shares under the 2015 Incentive Compensation Plan;
- 2,642 equity shares under the Infosys Expanded Stock Ownership Program 2019.

Consequently, on August 29, 2024, the issued and subscribed share capital of the Company stands increased to ₹ 20,76,06,73,405/- divided into 4,15,21,34,681 equity shares of face value of ₹ 5/- each.

This is for your information and records.

This will also be hosted on the Company's website, at www.infosys.com.

Yours Sincerely,

For Infosys Limited

A.G.S. Manikantha

Company Secretary ACS - 21918
