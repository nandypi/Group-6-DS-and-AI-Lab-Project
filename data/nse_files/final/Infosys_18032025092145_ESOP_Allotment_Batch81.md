## TO ALL STOCK EXCHANGES

## BSE LIMITED NATIONAL STOCK EXCHANGE OF INDIA LIMITED NEW YORK STOCK EXCHANGE

March 18, 2025

Dear Sir/Madam,

## Subject: Allotment of Equity Shares

This is to inform you that the Company has allotted 5,29,682 equity shares of face value of ₹ 5 each vide a Board resolution dated March 17, 2025, pursuant to the exercise of Restricted Stock Units by eligible employees as hereunder:

- 1,21,462 equity shares under the 2015 Incentive Compensation Plan;
- 4,08,220 equity shares under the Infosys Expanded Stock Ownership Program 2019.

Consequently, effective  from March  17,  2025,  the  issued  and  subscribed  share  capital of  the Company stands increased to ₹ 20,76,63,17,275/- divided into  4,15,32,63,455 equity  shares  of face value of ₹ 5/- each.

This is for your information and records.

This will also be hosted on the Company's website, at www.infosys.com.

Yours Sincerely,

For Infosys Limited

## A.G.S. Manikantha

Company Secretary Membership No. A21918
