## TO ALL STOCK EXCHANGES

## BSE LIMITED NATIONAL STOCK EXCHANGE OF INDIA LIMITED NEW YORK STOCK EXCHANGE

August 30, 2025

Dear Sir/Madam,

## Subject: Allotment of Equity Shares

This is to inform you that the Company has allotted 21,924 equity shares of face value of ₹ 5 each vide a Board resolution dated August 28, 2025, and approved on August 30, 2025, pursuant to the exercise of Restricted Stock Units by eligible employees as hereunder:

- 5,062 equity shares under the 2015 Incentive Compensation Plan;
- 16,862 equity shares under the Infosys Expanded Stock Ownership Program 2019.

Consequently,  effective  from  August  30,  2025,  the  issued  and  subscribed  share  capital  of  the Company stands increased to ₹ 20,77,19,61,015/- divided into 4,15,43,92,203 equity shares of face value of ₹ 5/- each.

This is for your information and records.

This will also be hosted on the Company's website, at www.infosys.com.

Yours Sincerely, For Infosys Limited

## A.G.S. Manikantha

Company Secretary Membership No. A21918
