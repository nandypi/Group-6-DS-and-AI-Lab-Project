## TO ALL STOCK EXCHANGES

## BSE LIMITED NATIONAL STOCK EXCHANGE OF INDIA LIMITED NEW YORK STOCK EXCHANGEA

December 19, 2025

Dear Sir/Madam,

## Subject: Allotment of Equity Shares

This is to inform you that the Company has allotted 43,490 equity shares of face value of ₹ 5 each vide a Board resolution dated December 17, 2025, and approved on December 19, 2025, pursuant to the exercise of Restricted Stock Units by eligible employees as hereunder:

- 17,672 equity shares under the 2015 Incentive Compensation Plan;
- 25,818 equity shares under the Infosys Expanded Stock Ownership Program 2019.

Consequently, effective from  December 19, 2025, the issued and subscribed share capital of the Company stands increased to ₹ 20,27,33,39,495/- divided into 4,05,46,67,899 equity shares of face value of ₹ 5/- each.

This is for your information and records.

This will also be hosted on the Company's website, at www.infosys.com.

Yours Sincerely, For

Infosys Limited

## A.G.S. Manikantha
