## TO ALL STOCK EXCHANGES

## BSE LIMITED NATIONAL STOCK EXCHANGE OF INDIA LIMITED NEW YORK STOCK EXCHANGE

September 19, 2024

Dear Sir/Madam,

## Subject: Company Statement

This is to inform that, Infosys Limited has agreed to invest in SpaceTech startup, GalaxEye up to INR 17 Crores (approximately US$ 2.0 million) as part of Infosys Innovation Fund. The details of investment as required under Regulation 30 of SEBI (Listing Obligations and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 2015 is enclosed as Annexure A.

This is for your information and records.

This information will also be hosted on the Company's website, at www.infosys.com.

Infosys Limited

Yours Sincerely, For

## A.G.S. Manikantha

Company Secretary ACS - 21918
