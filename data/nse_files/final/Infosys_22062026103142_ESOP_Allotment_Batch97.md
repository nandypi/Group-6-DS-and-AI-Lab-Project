## TO ALL STOCK EXCHANGES

## BSE LIMITED NATIONAL STOCK EXCHANGE OF INDIA LIMITED NEW YORK STOCK EXCHANGE

June 22, 2026

Dear Sir/Madam,

## Subject: Allotment of Equity Shares

This is to inform you that the Company has allotted 3,22,925 equity shares of face value of ₹ 5 each vide a Board resolution dated June 19, 2026, and approved on June 21, 2026, pursuant to the exercise of Restricted Stock Units by eligible employees as hereunder:

- 13,265 equity shares under the 2015 Incentive Compensation Plan;
- 3,09,660 equity shares under the Infosys Expanded Stock Ownership Program 2019.

Consequently, effective from June 21, 2026, the issued and subscribed share capital of the Company stands increased to ₹ 20,28,78,94,150/- divided into 4,05,75,78,830 equity shares of face value of 5/- each.

This is for your information and records.

This will also be hosted on the Company's website, at www.infosys.com.

Yours Sincerely, For

Infosys Limited
