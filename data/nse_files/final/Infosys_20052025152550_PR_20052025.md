## PRESS RELEASE Infosys Positioned as a Leader in Modern Application Development Services by

## Independent Research Firm

Bengaluru, India - May 20, 2025: Infosys (NSE, BSE, NYSE: INFY), a global leader in next-generation digital services and consulting, today announced that it has been positioned as a Leader in The Forrester Wave™:  Modern  Application  Development  Services,  Q1  2025.  Infosys  has  been  recognized  for  its innovation, global delivery capabilities, and robust partner ecosystem. In  this  assessment,  Forrester  evaluated  the  13  most  significant  players  that  offer  Modern  Application Development services across 25 criteria under two high-level categories - current offering and strategy. Infosys received the top score in the current offering category, and the highest scores possible in 14 out of 25  criteria,  including  partner  ecosystem,  AI  and  data  governance/engineering,  cloud-native  and  hybrid development services. Infosys is cited for its capabilities in agile development and platform engineering, with a focus on productbased  services.  The  report  also  states  that  Infosys  excels  at  fostering  deep  collaborations  with hyperscalers, product vendors, startups, and cloud service providers to build customized client solutions. Infosys leverages Infosys Topaz and Infosys Cobalt offerings to help businesses drive enterprise-scale modernization and innovative product development. It offers horizontal and domain solutions such as the Infosys Live Enterprise Application Development Platform,  Infosys Live Enterprise Automation Platform, Infosys Equinox and Infosys Helix to accelerate Modern Application Development programs. Diego Lo Giudice, Vice President, Principal Analyst, Forrester Research , writes in the report, 'Infosys' strategy heavily relies on a robust partner ecosystem, unparalleled among its peers. The company stands out  with  its  Modern  Application  Development  global  staffing  model,  benefiting  from  a  widespread international  presence.  Infosys  particularly  excels  in  AI,  showcasing  exceptional  strengths  across dimensions, such as its TuringBots accelerators, generative AI architecture, sustainable and ethical AI, AI testing,  and  AIIA  development.  Clients  looking  for  enterprise-scale  Modern  Application  Development delivery for innovative products and modernization of product development ways of working should partner with Infosys.' Balakrishna D. R. (Bali), Executive Vice President, Global Services Head, AI and Industry Verticals, Infosys ,  said,  'For  us,  being  recognized  as  a  Leader  in  the  Forrester  Wave™:  Modern  Application

Development Services underscores our commitment to fostering a thriving partner ecosystem and driving client success. Our proactive approach - bringing fresh insights and enabling strategic innovation with the combined capabilities of Infosys Topaz and Infosys Cobalt - defines how we deliver tailored solutions at scale. We believe this acknowledgment reflects our ability to harness world-class engineering and cuttingedge AI capabilities to deliver impactful solutions and empower businesses on their growth journeys.'
