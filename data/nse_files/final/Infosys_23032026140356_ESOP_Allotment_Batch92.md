## TO ALL STOCK EXCHANGES

## BSE LIMITED NATIONAL STOCK EXCHANGE OF INDIA LIMITED NEW YORK STOCK EXCHANGE

March 23, 2026

Dear Sir/Madam,

## Subject: Allotment of Equity Shares

This is to inform you that the Company has allotted 2,00,596 equity shares of face value of ₹ 5 each vide a Board resolution dated March 18, 2026, and approved on March 23, 2026, pursuant to the exercise of Restricted Stock Units by eligible employees as hereunder:

- 35,549 equity shares under the 2015 Incentive Compensation Plan;
- 1,65,047 equity shares under the Infosys Expanded Stock Ownership Program 2019.

Consequently, effective from March 23, 2026, the issued and subscribed share capital of the Company stands increased to ₹ 20,27,79,58,615/- divided into 4,05,55,91,723 equity shares of face value of ₹ 5/- each.

This is for your information and records.

This will also be hosted on the Company's website, at www.infosys.com.

Yours Sincerely, For

Infosys Limited
