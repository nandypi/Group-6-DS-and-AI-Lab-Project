The Board, on January 16, 2025, based on the recommendations of the Nomination and Remuneration Committee, approved the annual time-based stock incentives in the form of Restricted Stock Units (RSUs) to Salil Parekh, CEO &amp; MD having a market value of ₹3 crore as on the date of grant under the 2015 Stock Incentive Compensation Plan (2015 Plan) in accordance with the terms of his employment agreement. The RSUs will vest in line with the employment agreement. The RSUs will be granted w.e.f February 1, 2025 and the number of RSUs will be calculated based on the market price at the close of trading on a date immediately preceding the grant date. The exercise price of RSUs will be equal to the par value of the share.
