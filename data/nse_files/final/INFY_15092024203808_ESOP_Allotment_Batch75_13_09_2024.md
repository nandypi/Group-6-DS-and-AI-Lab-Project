## TO ALL STOCK EXCHANGES

## BSE LIMITED NATIONAL STOCK EXCHANGE OF INDIA LIMITED NEW YORK STOCK EXCHANGE

September 15, 2024

Dear Sir/Madam,

## Subject: Allotment of Equity Shares

This is to inform you that the Company has allotted 12,136 equity shares of face value of ₹ 5 each vide a Board resolution dated September 13, 2024 and approved on September 14, 2024, pursuant to the exercise of Restricted Stock Units by eligible employees as hereunder:

-  263 equity shares under the 2015 Incentive Compensation Plan;
-  11,873 equity shares under the Infosys Expanded Stock Ownership Program 2019.

Consequently,  on  September 14, 2024, the  issued  and  subscribed  share  capital  of  the  Company stands increased to ₹ 20,76,07,34,085/- divided into 4,15,21,46,817 equity shares of face value of ₹ 5/- each.

This is for your information and records.

This will also be hosted on the Company's website, at www.infosys.com.

Yours Sincerely, For Infosys Limited

A.G.S. Manikantha

Company Secretary
