## TO ALL STOCK EXCHANGES

## BSE LIMITED NATIONAL STOCK EXCHANGE OF INDIA LIMITED NEW YORK STOCK EXCHANGE

September 16, 2025

Dear Sir/ Madam,

## Sub: Trading window closure intimation

This is to inform you that the Company has closed the trading window for the earnings release of the quarter ending September 30, 2025 in compliance with SEBI (Prohibition of Insider Trading) Regulations, 2015, as amended, from September 16, 2025. The trading window will re-open after 48 hours of dissemination of the financial results of the Company.

This will also be hosted on the Company's website, at www.infosys.com

This is for your information and records.

Yours Sincerely, For Infosys Limited

## A.G.S. Manikantha
