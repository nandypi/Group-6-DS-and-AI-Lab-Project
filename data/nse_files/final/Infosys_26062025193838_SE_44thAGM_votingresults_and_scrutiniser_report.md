## Sub: Scrutinizer's report &amp; Voting results of 44 th  Annual General Meeting ('AGM')

The 44 th  AGM of the Company was held on June 25, 2025, through video conferencing and other audio-visual means, and the business mentioned in the Notice dated April 17, 2025, was transacted.

In this regard, please find enclosed the following:

1.  Voting results as required  under Regulation 44 of the SEBI (Listing Obligations and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 2015. All resolutions as set out in the notice of 44 th  AGM are passed with requisite majority
