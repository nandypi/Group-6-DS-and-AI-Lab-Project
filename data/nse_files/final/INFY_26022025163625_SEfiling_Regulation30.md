## TO ALL STOCK EXCHANGES

## BSE LIMITED NATIONAL STOCK EXCHANGE OF INDIA LIMITED NEW YORK STOCK EXCHANGE

February 26, 2025

Dear Sir/ Madam,

## Sub: Company update

This is to inform that, the Board of Infosys BPM UK Limited, a step-down wholly owned subsidiary of Infosys Limited and a wholly owned subsidiary of Infosys BPM Limited has approved to incorporate a wholly-owned subsidiary in the name and style of Infosys BPM Netherlands B.V (or by any other name as available in the commercial register) in the Netherlands.  Additional information as required under Regulation 30 of SEBI (Listing Obligations and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations will be disclosed in due course.

This  is  for  your  information  and  records.  The  same  will  also  be  hosted  on  the  Company's  website,  at www.infosys.com

Thanking you,

Yours sincerely,

For Infosys Limited

A.G.S. Manikantha Company Secretary
