## TO ALL STOCK EXCHANGES

## BSE LIMITED NATIONAL STOCK EXCHANGE OF INDIA LIMITED NEW YORK STOCK EXCHANGE

December 14, 2024

Dear Sir/Madam,

## Subject: Allotment of Equity Shares

This is to inform you that the Company has allotted 22,274 equity shares of face value of ₹ 5 each vide a Board resolution dated December 13, 2024 and approved on December 14, 2024, pursuant to the exercise of Restricted Stock Units by eligible employees as hereunder:

-  13,404 equity shares under the 2015 Incentive Compensation Plan;
-  8,870 equity shares under the Infosys Expanded Stock Ownership Program 2019.

Consequently,  effective  from  December  14,  2024,  the  issued  and  subscribed  share  capital  of  the Company stands increased to ₹ 20,76,13,45,970/- divided into 4,15,22,69,194 equity shares of face value of ₹ 5/- each.

This is for your information and records.

This will also be hosted on the Company's website, at www.infosys.com.

Yours Sincerely, For Infosys Limited

## A.G.S. Manikantha

Company Secretary Membership No. ACS 21918
