## TO ALL STOCK EXCHANGES

## BSE LIMITED NATIONAL STOCK EXCHANGE OF INDIA LIMITED NEW YORK STOCK EXCHANGE

February 15, 2025

Dear Sir/Madam,

## Subject: Allotment of Equity Shares

This is to inform you that the Company has allotted 4,59,027 equity shares of face value of ₹ 5 each vide a Board resolution dated February 12, 2025 and approved on February 14, 2025, pursuant to the exercise of Restricted Stock Units by eligible employees as hereunder:

- 2,32,254 equity shares under the 2015 Incentive Compensation Plan;
- 2,26,773 equity shares under the Infosys Expanded Stock Ownership Program 2019.

Consequently, effective from February 14, 2025, the issued and  subscribed share capital  of the Company stands increased to ₹ 20,76,36,68,865/- divided into 4,15,27,33,773 equity shares of face value of ₹ 5/- each.

This is for your information and records.

This will also be hosted on the Company's website, at www.infosys.com.

Yours Sincerely, For

Infosys Limited
