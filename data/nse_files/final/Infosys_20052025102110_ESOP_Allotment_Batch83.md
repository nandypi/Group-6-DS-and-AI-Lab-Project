## TO ALL STOCK EXCHANGES

## BSE LIMITED NATIONAL STOCK EXCHANGE OF INDIA LIMITED NEW YORK STOCK EXCHANGE

May 20, 2025

Dear Sir/Madam,

## Subject: Allotment of Equity Shares

This is to inform you that the Company has allotted 3,18,079 equity shares of face value of ₹ 5 each vide a Board resolution May 19, 2025, and approved on May 20, 2025, pursuant to the exercise of Restricted Stock Units by eligible employees as hereunder:

- 48,730
- 2,69,349 equity shares under the Infosys Expanded Stock Ownership Program 2019.

Consequently, effective from May 20, 2025, the issued and subscribed share capital of the Company stands increased to ₹ 20,77,10,66,570/- divided into 4,15,42,13,314 equity shares of face value of ₹ 5/- each.

This is for your information and records.

This will also be hosted on the Company's website, at www.infosys.com.

Yours Sincerely, For Infosys Limited

## A.G.S. Manikantha

Company Secretary Membership No. A21918
