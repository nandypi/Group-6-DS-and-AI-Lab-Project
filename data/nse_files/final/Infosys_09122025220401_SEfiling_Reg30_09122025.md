## TO ALL STOCK EXCHANGES

## BSE LIMITED NATIONAL STOCK EXCHANGE OF INDIA LIMITED NEW YORK STOCK EXCHANGE

December 09, 2025

Dear Sir/ Madam,

## Sub: Company update

This is to inform that, Board of Infosys Singapore Pte Ltd, a wholly owned subsidiary of Infosys Limited has approved to incorporate a wholly owned subsidiary in the name and style of "Infosys Vietnam LLC'. (or by any other name as available in the corporate registry) in Vietnam. Additional information as required under Regulation 30 of SEBI (Listing Obligations and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 2015 will be disclosed in due course.

This is for your information and records. The same will also be hosted on the Company's website, at www.infosys.com

Thanking you, Yours sincerely,
