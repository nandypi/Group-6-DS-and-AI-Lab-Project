## TO ALL STOCK EXCHANGES

## BSE LIMITED NATIONAL STOCK EXCHANGE OF INDIA LIMITED NEW YORK STOCK EXCHANGE

Dear Sir/ Madam,

Sub: Intimation  under  Regulation  30  of  SEBI  (Listing  Obligations  and  Disclosure  Requirements) Regulations, 2015 ('Listing Regulations')

This is to inform that the Company has agreed with UVC Partners (www.uvcpartners.com), a Venture Capital fund based out of Germany, committing to invest €5.0 million.

The details as required under Regulation 30 of Listing Regulations is appended herewith.

This is for your information and records.

This will also be hosted on the Company's website, at www.infosys.com
