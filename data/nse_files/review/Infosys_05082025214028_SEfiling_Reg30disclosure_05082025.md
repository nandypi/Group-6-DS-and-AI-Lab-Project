## Sub:  Update on McCamish Cyber Incident

In continuation to our statement dated March 14, 2025, we would like to update that on August 5, 2025 , Infosys McCamish Systems (' IMS '), a subsidiary of Infosys BPM limited (a wholly owned subsidiary of Infosys limited) and the State of Oregon (United States of America) Attorney's General Office entered into an Assurance of Voluntary Compliance (' AVC ').

The Oregon Attorney's General Office expressed concerns that IMS may have violated the Oregon Consumer Information Protection Act, and the Oregon Unlawful Trade Practices Act with respect to a 2023 data breach.

IMS has entered into an Assurance of Voluntary Compliance to resolve this matter without any further action. The AVC specifically acknowledges that this is not an admission or finding that IMS violated the Acts as listed above, and that IMS does not admit any of the factual assertions contained in the AVC.
