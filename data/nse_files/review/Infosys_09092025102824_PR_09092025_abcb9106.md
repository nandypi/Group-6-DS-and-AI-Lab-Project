## PRESS RELEASE

## Bank CTBC Indonesia Adopts Infosys Finacle Digital Banking Solution on Cloud

Bengaluru, India - September 9, 2025 :  Infosys Finacle, part of EdgeVerve Systems, a wholly-owned subsidiary of Infosys (NSE, BSE, NYSE: INFY), and PT Bank CTBC Indonesia (Bank CTBC Indonesia), subsidiary of the CTBC Group, Taiwan, today announced the successful deployment of Finacle Digital Banking Solution Suite on a cloud hosted model, as part of the bank's digital transformation initiatives. With this  deployment,  the  bank  can  now  scale  effortlessly  to  meet  growing  business  demands  and  deliver innovations  faster,  ensuring  shorter  time-to-market  for  new  products  and  services.  This  modernization aligns with the bank's goal of improving operational efficiency and enhancing customer experience.

The bank has upgraded to the latest version of Finacle loans, deposits, trade finance, payments, and customer data hub as part of this adoption. These solutions are seamlessly integrated with the Bank CTBC Indonesia's existing application landscape, which also includes the Finacle Online Banking and the Finacle Retail Loan Origination solutions.

## Key Highlights:

- The Finacle Digital Banking Solution Suite on cloud enables the bank to accelerate new feature releases and foster rapid innovation, ensuring improved time-to-market for new offerings while maintaining robust security.
- The Finacle Customer Data Hub gives the bank a unified, 360-degree view of customer data, enabling hyper personalization of services and seamless third-party integration for allied offerings.
- The  bank  has  seen  transformative  performance  gains  by  opting  for  the  Finacle  suite  on  a PostgreSQL database platform. The combined solution has resulted in over 300% reduction in bulk processing time, significantly enabling operational efficiency, and reduced total cost of ownership (TCO).

Iwan Satawidinata, President Director, PT Bank CTBC Indonesia , said, 'Our collaboration with Infosys Finacle has set a new bar for digital banking in Indonesia. The deployment of the new Infosys Finacle solutions represents a pivotal moment in our bank's evolution and a cornerstone of our broader 'Journey Next' strategy, focused on driving digital innovation and operational excellence. These advanced solutions position us at the forefront of Indonesia's banking sector, empowering us to deliver secure, tailored offerings that address our customers' evolving needs. We are excited about the future possibilities this collaboration brings, not only for our business but also for our stakeholders and the communities we serve.'

<!-- image -->

Sajit Vijayakumar, Chief Executive Officer, Infosys Finacle , said, 'This deployment is a testament to the power of our cloud-native digital banking solutions in enabling bank-wide transformation. Bank CTBC Indonesia stands to gain the benefits of secure, scalable flexibility with the deployment on cloud and is wellpositioned  to  deliver  an  industry  leading  digital  banking  platform  to  its  customers.  This  collaboration underscores our shared commitment to help the bank drive innovation and inspire better banking with exceptional customer experiences.'

## About Bank CTBC Indonesia

PT Bank CTBC Indonesia (formerly named PT Bank Chinatrust Indonesia) is 99% shareholder owned by CTBC Bank Co., Ltd. (formerly known as Chinatrust Commercial Bank Co., Ltd.), one of the leading banks in  Taiwan  and  the  remaining  1%  is  owned  by  PT  Bank  Danamon  Indonesia  Tbk,  one  of  the  premier Indonesian banks. PT Bank CTBC Indonesia ('Bank CTBC Indonesia') started its operation in Indonesia in mid.1997, mainly to serve the Indo-Taiwanese trade, under the name of PT Bank Chinatrust Tamara with shareholding of 85% by Chinatrust Commercial Bank and 15% by Bank Tamara. In year 2001 Chinatrust Commercial Bank increased its ownership to 99% and changed the Bank's name to PT Bank Chinatrust Indonesia. And in 2013, Chinatrust Commercial Bank Co., Ltd. as majority shareholder of Bank CTBC Indonesia had changed its name to CTBC Bank Co., Ltd., PT Bank Chinatrust Indonesia then followed with name change to Bank CTBC Indonesia in the same year.

With a strong capital base and consistent performance since its establishment, Bank CTBC Indonesia has been recognized as one of the leading banks in Indonesia. Bank CTBC Indonesia provides a full range of corporate and consumer services in Indonesia and is committed to become a bank that can be relied upon and  trusted.  With  assets  totaling  IDR  22.86  trillion,  and  11  (eleven)  offices  in  Indonesia.  Bank  CTBC Indonesia business activities continue to focus on developing cooperation and long-term relationships with customers.

PT  Bank  CTBC  Indonesia  is  registered  and  supervised  by  Financial  Services  Authority  (OJK),  Bank Indonesia (BI) and also a participant of Indonesia Deposit Insurance Corporation (LPS) guarantee program
