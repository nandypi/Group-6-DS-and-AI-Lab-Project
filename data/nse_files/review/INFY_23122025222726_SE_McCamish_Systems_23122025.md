Dear Sir/ Madam,

## Sub: Update on McCamish Cyber Incident

In continuation to our statement dated March 14, 2025, we would like to update that on December 22, 2025,  Infosys  McCamish  Systems  (' IMS '),  a  subsidiary  of  Infosys  BPM  limited  (a  wholly  owned subsidiary of  Infosys  limited)  and  the  State  of  South  Dakota  (United  States  of  America)  Division  of Insurance entered into a Consent Order.

The South Dakota Division of Insurance expressed concerns that IMS may have violated South Dakota Codified Laws related to notification with respect to a 2023 data breach.

IMS has entered into a Consent Order to resolve this matter without any further action. The Consent Order specifically acknowledges that this is not an admission that IMS violated the Acts as listed above.
